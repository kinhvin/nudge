# About the Project
Nudge is the habit tracker that doesn't sit around and wait for you, it 'nudges' you periodically to ensure you stay on top of the habits you want to have or even break. 
On top of that, it will keep track of your day-to-day progress through the very familiar GitHub-style contributions graph. 
<br><br/>
**Note this is an active development, some features mentioned here have not been implemented yet, but are planned to be.**
# Inspiration
As someone who both wants to start new habits and also stop habits, having someone to keep you accountable is always great. So, the whole idea with nudge is for it to be
'who' keeps you accountable. It won't feel bad for keeping you accountable and you can set how frequently it will nudge you. As well, I decided to integrate it across
platforms (mostly) everyone is familiar with nowadays - Discord and Telegram.
